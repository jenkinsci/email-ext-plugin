Place a check next to each group of people you would like to send emails to if the trigger is triggered.
  • Recipients - those listed in the recipients list at the project level.
  • Developers - those who made changes to the code.
  • Requestor - the person who initiated the build, if a person (not SCM polling or hook).
  • Culprits - used in conjunction with Developers, gets the culprits from Jenkins itself rather than the changeset.
